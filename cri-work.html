Uber RFP Responses

2017 / 2018

Role
  • Graphic Designer
  • Motion Designer

Coordinated Resources had the opportunity to bid for Uber's new Bay Area office move. I designed the response package which included a digital presentation as well as an accompanying motion graphic to make CRI stand out from its competitors. In the end, Uber never went through with the move, but our RFP package was the choice pick due to the visuals and the attention to detail within the package.
